‚ú¶ Under the Hood

üß† How It Works

01

Capture

OpenCV opens the default webcam and reads a continuous stream of frames in real time.

02

Preprocess

Each frame is converted to grayscale to reduce computational load and improve detection accuracy.

03

Detect

The Haar Cascade classifier scans the grayscale frame, identifying regions that match frontal face patterns.

04

Render

Bounding boxes are drawn in green around every detected face on the original full-color frame.

05

Display

The annotated frame is pushed to an OpenCV window and rendered live to the screen.

06

Loop

Steps 1‚Äì5 repeat for every frame at camera frame rate until the user presses Q to exit.
